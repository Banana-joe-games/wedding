The Venue

Villa Walter
Fontana

Designed in the mid-1960s by architect Giacomo Mantegazza as a private residence, Villa Walter Fontana is a prestigious estate spanning 2,000 sqm, surrounded by a centuries-old park.

Walter Fontana's passion for art is still visible today in the many works adorning the park and the villa.
